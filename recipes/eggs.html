Scrambled Eggs Recipe

Description

Scrambled huevos.

Ingredients

  • Whole eggs
  • Egg whites
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Turkey breast
  • Green pepper
  • Shredded cheese
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Crack 2 whole eggs and whisk with 2 servings of egg whites
  2. Chop onions and green pepper. Cook until onion caramelizes.
  3. Add egg mix
  4. Season with salt and pepper
  5. Add chopped turkey breast
  6. Add shredded cheese
  7. Mix
  8. Indulge
