Ribeye Steak Airfryer Recipe

Description

The following ribeye recipe is quick to make and delicious to eat.

Ingredients

  • Ribeye
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Powdered Garilic

Steps

  1. Rub olive oil onto thawed ribeye steak
  2. Apply seasoning (salt, pepper, garlic, to taste)
  3. Cook in airfyer for 6 minutes at 400F
  4. Flip ribeye and cook for another 5 minutes at 400F
  5. Remove ribeye from airfyer and let rest for 5 minutes
  6. Indulge
